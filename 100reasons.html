✦ A prayer for us

From my heart to God

Lord, thank You for My Love.
For her soft heart, her care for others,
her faith that shines through everything she does.

Thank You for using her to pull me
out of a season I couldn't find my way through alone.
Thank You for making her loyal and steady
and full of a love that noticed the little things.

I pray You protect her completely —
her heart, her peace, her path.
I pray she always feels seen, chosen, and safe.
That she knows she is worth every slow, gentle moment.

Make me the man she deserves.
Rooted in You. Soft for her.
Constant in the small things that make her feel loved.

Thank You for our picnic, for our laughs,
for every forehead kiss and every door I got to open.
Let there be thousands more.

Cover us in Your grace
all the way to January 9th —
and every day after that. Amen
